<p><p>Semi-intensive green roofs are categorized into two market types: common and custom. Common type roofs are pre-designed systems with standardized components, making them cost-effective and simpler to install. They typically feature a variety of plant species suited for moderate maintenance. On the other hand, custom type green roofs are tailored to specific architectural designs and client preferences, allowing for greater diversity in plant selection and layout, but usually requiring more complex installation and maintenance strategies.</p></p>
