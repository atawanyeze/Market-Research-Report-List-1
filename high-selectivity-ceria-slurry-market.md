<p><p>The High Selectivity Ceria Slurry market consists of two primary types: Calcined Ceria Slurry and Colloidal Ceria Slurry. Calcined Ceria Slurry is produced by heating cerium oxide to enhance its properties for polishing applications, making it effective in semiconductor and optical industries. In contrast, Colloidal Ceria Slurry features fine nanoparticles suspended in a liquid medium, offering excellent dispersion and stability. This type is widely utilized for more delicate polishing tasks due to its high surface area and uniformity.</p></p>
