<p><p>5G Non-Standalone (NSA) architecture leverages existing 4G infrastructure while introducing new components to enhance connectivity. </p><p>- **Femtocells** are small, low-power nodes designed for home or small office use, improving coverage in localized areas. </p><p>- **Pico cells** serve larger spaces like shopping malls, enhancing urban connectivity.</p><p>- **Micro cells** cover medium-sized areas, ideal for dense urban environments. </p><p>- **Macro cells** provide extensive coverage over wide regions, supporting the backbone of the network. Together, these types enable efficient 5G deployment and improved user experiences.</p></p>
