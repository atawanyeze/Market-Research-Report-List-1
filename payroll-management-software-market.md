<p><p>Payroll management software can be categorized into three primary types: on-premise, Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), and cloud-hosted solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering full control but requiring maintenance and updates. SaaS delivers payroll services via the internet, allowing easy access while reducing the need for infrastructure. Cloud-hosted solutions combine benefits of both by providing a web-based platform with scalable storage and functions, ensuring accessibility and flexibility for users without extensive IT management.</p></p>
