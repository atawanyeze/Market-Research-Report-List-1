<p><p>The snail automobile horn market comprises two main types: electronic horns and electromagnetic horns. Electronic horns utilize digital sound technology, producing a variety of tones with higher efficiency and often requiring less power, making them ideal for modern vehicles. In contrast, electromagnetic horns operate on traditional principles, using an electromagnet to create sound by vibrating a metal diaphragm. These horns are known for their robust sound output and reliability, making them popular in many vehicles. Both types serve essential safety functions in automotive design.</p></p>
