<p><p>The multispectral imaging system market is segmented into two main types: single lens and multi-camera systems. Single lens systems utilize one camera equipped with multiple spectral bands, providing a compact solution suitable for various applications like agriculture and environmental monitoring. In contrast, multi-camera systems involve multiple cameras, each capturing different spectral bands simultaneously, allowing for higher resolution and more detailed imaging. These systems are particularly beneficial in advanced research and industrial applications, where precision and data richness are critical.</p></p>
