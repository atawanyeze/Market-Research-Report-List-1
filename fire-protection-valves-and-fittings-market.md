<p><p>The Fire Protection Valves and Fittings market encompasses various types tailored for specific fire suppression systems. Wet system valves, integral to sprinkler systems, maintain constant water flow. Dry system valves are crucial for environments where freezing is a risk, activating only when needed. Deluge and pre-action system valves are designed for rapid discharge in high-risk areas. General-purpose valves include pressure regulating valves, butterfly valves, trims, fail-safe valves, and check valves, each serving unique roles in ensuring system reliability and functionality.</p></p>
