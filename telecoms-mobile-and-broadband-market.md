<p><p>The telecoms market encompasses various broadband types, each offering distinct technologies. Cable broadband uses coaxial cable to deliver high-speed internet, while DSL (Digital Subscriber Line) utilizes existing telephone lines for connectivity. FTTP (Fiber to the Premises) provides the fastest speeds by directly connecting fiber optic cables to homes. Wi-Fi offers wireless connectivity within a limited area, while WiMAX (Worldwide Interoperability for Microwave Access) extends broadband over longer distances wirelessly. Together, these technologies cater to diverse user needs and geographical challenges.</p></p>
