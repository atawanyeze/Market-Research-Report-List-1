<p><strong>The Small Size Panel Display Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Source Drive IC</li><li>Gate Drive IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Small Size Panel Display Driver IC market is categorized into Source Drive ICs and Gate Drive ICs. Source Drive ICs manage the flow of signals to individual pixels, directly controlling brightness and color, essential for high-quality displays. In contrast, Gate Drive ICs control the timing and sequence for refreshing pixels in a display panel, ensuring effective scanning and image stability. Together, these ICs play a critical role in enhancing the performance, efficiency, and visual quality of small-sized display panels.</p></p>
