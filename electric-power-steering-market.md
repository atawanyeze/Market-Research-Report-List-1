<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li><li>REPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) market comprises three main types: Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS), Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS), and Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS). CEPS utilizes an electric motor mounted on the steering column, offering precise control and compact design. PEPS is integrated into the steering pinion, providing enhanced steering response and efficiency. REPS, situated on the steering rack, delivers superior feedback and greater energy efficiency, making it popular in modern vehicles for improved performance and safety.</p></p>
