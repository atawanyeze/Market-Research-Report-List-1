<p><p>The microbial compound fertilizer market encompasses various types based on their beneficial microbial functions. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers contain bacteria that convert atmospheric nitrogen into forms usable by plants, enhancing soil fertility. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers contain microorganisms that mobilize phosphorus, making it more accessible to crops. Other types may include those promoting potassium solubilization, organic matter decomposition, or enhancing plant resilience to stress. Together, these microbial products improve soil health and optimize nutrient availability, leading to sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
