<p><p>The truck-mounted forklifts market comprises primarily two types: mast type and telescopic type. Mast type forklifts feature a standard vertical mast for lifting, offering stability and straightforward operation, making them ideal for confined spaces. In contrast, telescopic type forklifts have an extendable boom that allows for increased outreach and versatility, enabling them to handle loads at greater heights and distances. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing efficiency in material handling and logistics operations.</p></p>
