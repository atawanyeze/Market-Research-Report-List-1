<p><ul><li>Poly (styrene-butadiene-styrene) (SBS) Type</li><li>Poly (styrene-isoprene-styrene) (SIS) Type</li><li>Poly (styrene-ethylene/butylene-styrene) (SEBS) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Styrenic Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) market encompasses three main types: Poly (styrene-butadiene-styrene) (SBS), Poly (styrene-isoprene-styrene) (SIS), and Poly (styrene-ethylene/butylene-styrene) (SEBS). SBS provides excellent elasticity and resilience, making it suitable for applications like adhesives and sealants. SIS offers superior thermal stability and softness, ideal for consumer products. SEBS, known for its enhanced weather resistance and UV stability, is often used in more demanding applications, including automotive and medical devices, due to its versatile properties.</p></p>
