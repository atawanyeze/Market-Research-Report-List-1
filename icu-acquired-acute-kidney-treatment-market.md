<p><strong>The ICU-acquired Acute Kidney Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-renal</li><li>Intrinsic-renal</li><li>Post-renal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ICU-acquired Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) treatment market is categorized into three types: pre-renal, intrinsic-renal, and post-renal. Pre-renal AKI arises from inadequate blood flow to the kidneys, often due to dehydration or low blood pressure. Intrinsic-renal AKI results from direct damage to kidney tissues, often caused by toxins or infections. Post-renal AKI is due to obstruction in the urinary tract, hindering urine flow. Each category requires specific treatment approaches and influences market demand based on underlying causes and patient demographics.</p></p>
