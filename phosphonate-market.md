<p><ul><li>ATMP</li><li>HEDP</li><li>DTPMP</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphonate market primarily consists of various types, including ATMP (Amino Trimethylene Phosphonic Acid), HEDP (Hydroxyethylidene Diphosphonic Acid), and DTPMP (Diethylenetriamine Penta (Methylene Phosphonic Acid)). ATMP is used for scale and corrosion inhibition in water treatment, while HEDP is effective for metal ion chelation. DTPMP serves similar purposes but offers enhanced stability in complex environments. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses various specialized phosphonates used in agriculture, textiles, and other industrial applications, contributing to diverse market dynamics.</p></p>
