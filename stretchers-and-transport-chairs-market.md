<p><p>The market for stretchers and transport chairs can be categorized into three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual stretchers and chairs require physical effort for operation, making them cost-effective and easy to maintain. Electric models are powered by batteries or electricity, providing ease of use and reduced strain for caregivers. Hydraulic options utilize a pumping mechanism for height adjustment and easier patient transfer, offering a balance between manual effort and powered assistance, catering to diverse healthcare needs.</p></p>
