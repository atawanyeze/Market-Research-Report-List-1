<p><strong>The Iron Oxide Target Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iron oxide target market comprises two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, stationary surfaces used in sputtering processes to deposit thin films of iron oxide on substrates. They offer uniform coatings and are commonly employed in electronics and optics. In contrast, rotating targets involve a cylindrical design that spins during the deposition process. This rotation provides enhanced material distribution and efficiency, making them favorable for larger-scale production and applications requiring consistent coating thickness.</p></p>
