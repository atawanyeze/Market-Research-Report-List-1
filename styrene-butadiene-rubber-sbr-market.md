<p><p>Styrene Butadiene Rubber (SBR) is a synthetic rubber widely used in various applications, particularly in tires. The SBR market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **SBR** refers to the general type used in standard applications.</p><p>2. **E-SBR (Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Rubber)** is produced via emulsion polymerization, offering better processability and lower viscosity. </p><p>3. **S-SBR (Solution Styrene Butadiene Rubber)** is created through solution polymerization, providing superior performance properties in high-performance tire formulations. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
