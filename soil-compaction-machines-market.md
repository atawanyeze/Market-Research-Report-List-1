<p><p>The soil compaction machines market is divided into two primary types: heavy and light compaction machines. Heavy compaction machines, such as vibratory rollers and large tamping machines, are used for large-scale projects requiring deep compaction, often in construction and infrastructure. Light compaction machines, such as plate compactors and rammer compactors, are designed for smaller jobs, providing effective compaction in restricted areas and for landscaping. Both types play essential roles in ensuring soil stability and density across various applications.</p></p>
