<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spring cylinder linear actuator market is primarily segmented into two types: single acting and double acting actuators. Single acting actuators utilize a spring mechanism to return to their original position after an external force is applied, making them suitable for applications requiring simple, cost-effective solutions. In contrast, double acting actuators provide movement in both directions—extending and retracting—through air or hydraulic pressure, offering greater control and power, ideal for more complex and demanding applications.</p></p>
