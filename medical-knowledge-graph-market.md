<p><p>The Medical Knowledge Graph market encompasses two primary types: Structured and Unstructured Knowledge Graphs. </p><p>Structured Knowledge Graphs organize medical information into well-defined schemas, utilizing relationships and attributes for easy querying and analysis. They enhance data interoperability and support clinical decision-making.</p><p>Unstructured Knowledge Graphs, on the other hand, extract insights from unstructured data sources like clinical notes, research articles, and patient records. They leverage natural language processing to identify patterns and relationships, thereby facilitating deeper insights into patient care and drug development.</p></p>
