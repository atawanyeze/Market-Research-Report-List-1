<p><p>The interior wall putty powder market is primarily divided into two types: cement-based putty and gypsum-based putty. Cement-based putty is durable, moisture-resistant, and ideal for exterior surfaces, providing a strong base for finishes. It is suitable for humid climates. In contrast, gypsum-based putty offers a smoother finish and is preferred for interior applications due to its quick drying time and ease of application. Both types enhance the appearance and longevity of wall surfaces in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
