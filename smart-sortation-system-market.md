<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Sortation System market comprises Linear and Loop Sortation Systems, each offering distinct advantages for efficient item handling. Linear Sortation Systems operate on a straight path, making them ideal for high-speed sorting of small to medium-sized items in warehouses and distribution centers. In contrast, Loop Sortation Systems utilize a circular design, maximizing space and allowing for continuous flow, which is particularly effective for larger items and variable sorting needs. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and ensure timely order fulfillment.</p></p>
