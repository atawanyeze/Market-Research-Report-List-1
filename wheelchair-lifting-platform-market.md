<p><p>The wheelchair lifting platform market consists primarily of vertical platform lifts and incline platform lifts. Vertical platform lifts are designed to transport individuals vertically between different levels, often used in homes or public buildings where space for a traditional elevator is limited. Incline platform lifts, on the other hand, are designed to move wheelchair users along a slope or staircase, making them ideal for enhancing accessibility in spaces with existing stairs. Both types aim to improve mobility and independence for individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
