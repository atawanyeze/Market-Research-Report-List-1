<p><p>The Emergency Exit Lighting market is categorized into three primary types: </p><p>1. **Self-Contained Power System**: This type has individual battery units within each fixture, ensuring operation during power failures without external support.</p><p>2. **Central Power System**: It utilizes a centralized battery backup that powers multiple fixtures, offering streamlined maintenance and monitoring of emergency lighting.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Power System**: This combines elements of both self-contained and central systems, allowing for flexibility and improved reliability in emergency lighting applications across various environments.</p></p>
