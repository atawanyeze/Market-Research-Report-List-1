<p><strong>The SFP Fiber Optic Transceiver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode SFP Transceivers</li><li>Multi-mode SFP Transceivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SFP fiber optic transceiver market is divided into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode SFP transceivers. Single-mode SFP transceivers utilize a narrow optical fiber to transmit signals over long distances, typically exceeding 10 kilometers, making them ideal for wide-area networks. In contrast, multi-mode SFP transceivers use a broader optical fiber, allowing for shorter distance transmission, usually up to 2 kilometers, suitable for local area networks. Both types cater to different networking needs, balancing distance and data transmission rates.</p></p>
