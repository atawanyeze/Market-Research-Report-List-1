<p><strong>The Camera Module Assembly Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Curable Adhesives</li><li>Non-UV Curable Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera module assembly adhesives market comprises primarily two types: UV curable adhesives and non-UV curable adhesives. UV curable adhesives are cured quickly upon exposure to ultraviolet light, providing a strong bond and enhancing production efficiency. They are ideal for high-speed applications. Non-UV curable adhesives, on the other hand, rely on moisture, heat, or chemical reactions for curing, offering versatility in various environments but generally taking longer to set. Both types are essential for ensuring durability and performance in camera module assemblies.</p></p>
