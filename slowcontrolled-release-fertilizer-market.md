<p><p>The slow/controlled release fertilizer market consists of various types, primarily including binary compound fertilizers and NPK compound fertilizers. Binary compound fertilizers combine two essential nutrients, typically nitrogen and phosphorus, providing targeted nourishment to plants over an extended period. NPK compound fertilizers contain a balanced blend of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, promoting overall plant health and growth. These fertilizers release nutrients gradually, enhancing soil fertility and reducing the risk of nutrient leaching, thereby ensuring efficient and sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
