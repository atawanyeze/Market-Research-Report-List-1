<p><p>The personal dosimetry market encompasses various types of dosimeters used to measure an individual’s exposure to radiation. Thermoluminescent dosimeters (TLD) utilize heat to measure accumulated dose, while optically stimulated luminescent dosimeters (OSL) use light stimulation for analysis. Radiachrome personal dosimeters (RPL) are sensitive to radiation and provide real-time monitoring. Active dosimeters continuously record radiation exposure, offering immediate feedback. Each type caters to different monitoring needs in occupational health, medical fields, and environmental safety.</p></p>
