<p><p>The AR Geometric Waveguide Module market encompasses two main types: Reflector Input and Prism Input. Reflector Input utilizes mirrors to direct light, enhancing image clarity and field of view, making it ideal for applications requiring high visibility. In contrast, Prism Input employs prisms to manipulate light paths, which can reduce size and improve optical performance. Both types are integral in augmented reality systems, facilitating immersive user experiences through effective light management and display technologies.</p></p>
