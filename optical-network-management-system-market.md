<p><p>The Optical Network Management System (ONMS) market encompasses various testing types, primarily focusing on Peer-to-Peer (P2P) and Point-to-Multipoint (P2MP) networks. P2P network testing is essential for verifying direct connections between two endpoints, ensuring optimal performance and reliability in data transmission. Conversely, P2MP network testing evaluates the effectiveness of a single source communicating with multiple endpoints, essential for monitoring bandwidth and service quality. Both testing types are critical for maintaining robust and efficient optical network operations.</p></p>
