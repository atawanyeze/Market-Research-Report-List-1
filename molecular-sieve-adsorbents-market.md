<p><p>Molecular sieve adsorbents are categorized based on pore size and applications. Type 3A has a pore diameter of 3 angstroms, ideal for selectively adsorbing water and small gases. Type 4A, with 4 angstrom pores, can trap larger molecules like carbon dioxide. Type 5A, featuring 5 angstrom pores, is suited for larger molecules including hydrocarbons. Type X possesses flexible pore sizes, enhancing versatility in gas separation. Other types include specialized formulations for niche applications, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
