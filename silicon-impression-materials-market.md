<p><strong>The Silicon Impression Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Body</li><li>Medium to Heavy Body</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon impression materials market is categorized into two main types: light body and medium to heavy body materials. Light body materials are thinner and more fluid, allowing for detailed capturing of fine textures and anatomy in dental impressions. They are often used for precision work around margins. In contrast, medium to heavy body materials are thicker, providing more bulk and support, ideal for creating the overall structure of the impression. Both types are essential for accurate dental applications.</p></p>
